Improve your Bitcoin Experience

Securing Bitcoin payments since 2011, Electrum is one of the most popular Bitcoin wallets.

Electrum is fast, secure and easy to use. It suits the needs of a wide spectrum of users.

Do not trust. Verify.

Electrum verifies that your transactions are in the Bitcoin blockchain.

Bitcoin is not about trust, it’s about freedom and independence.

Free and Decentralized

Electrum is free software, released under the MIT License.

Anyone can run an Electrum server — no single entity controls the network.

Hardware Wallets

Multi-Platform
